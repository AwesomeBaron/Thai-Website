WHO WE ARE

Thai Agro & Allied Industries is organic oriented Agricultural Enterprise that has over the years raised awareness on the importance of agriculture in Nigeria and and empowered individuals, Churches, Co-operate entities and various government agencies with the neccessary skill to turn agriculture into a profitable venture and also be able to provide food for families.

OUR PURPOSE OF EXISTING

Our purpose is solidly ingrained in our mission and vision statements
MISSION We exist to build a food secured Nigeria/African through setting up farm, supporting and training local farmers with modern technology in partnership with Thai-Land

OUR VISION is to turn our fallow lands in Nigeria/African into bread, unlocking its treasures
